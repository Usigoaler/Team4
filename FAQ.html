Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know if my booking is confirmed?

Typically, when you complete the booking process on a flight booking website, you will receive a confirmation email that includes your itinerary and booking details. This email serves as confirmation of your booking. You can also log in to your account on the booking website to view your booking details and confirm that it has been processed successfully.

How do I book flights on your website?

Visit the ASTONAIR. Enter your travel dates, destination, and departure city or airport Choose the number of passengers and cabin class. Browse the available flights and select the one that suits you best. Enter the required passenger details, including full name, contact information, and payment details. Review your booking details, including the fare, taxes, and any additional fees or charges. Confirm your booking and wait for the confirmation email or itinerary to be sent to you.

Can I change or cancel my reservation? If so, how?

To change or cancel a flight reservation, log in to your account on the flight booking website, find your reservation, review the airline's policies, and follow the instructions to change or cancel your booking. Note that fees and refund policies will vary depending on the airline and ticket type.

no, then there are no additional fees or charges that you should be aware of when booking through the website.
